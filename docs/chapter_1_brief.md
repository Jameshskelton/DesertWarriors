# Chapter 1 Brief: Exile In The Greenwood

## Narrative Goal

Introduce George as an exiled prince under pursuit, establish the tone of survival on the run, and show that allies will gather around him as the campaign grows.

## Gameplay Goal

Teach the player to:

- use forests for avoid and defense
- protect George while advancing
- understand separate battle cutaways
- value class contrast between lord, knight, priest, cavalry, and mage

## Chapter Beats

1. Opening dialogue explains George's exile and immediate danger.
2. George, Bram, and Brother Hale begin on the southern road.
3. Rowan arrives as a turn-2 reinforcement from the southwest.
4. George can visit the village to recruit Ember.
5. Captain Briar anchors the enemy at the northern shrine.
6. Victory dialogue points toward the next chapter.

## Encounter Design

- Early brigands pressure the road approach.
- Hunters punish careless exposure on open tiles.
- The pursuer near the village creates the recruit challenge.
- The boss uses the shrine's defense bonus to force a deliberate finish.
